Xmas Countdown Template

Xmas Countdown is a modern Christmas countdown template featuring a sleek glassmorphism design with a dark tech aesthetic. The hero section displays a live countdown timer to December 25, 2025 at 6:00 PM, with animated floating particles and gentle snowfall effects. The template includes sections for About, Events, Traditions, and a Newsletter subscription form � all styled with frosted glass cards, gradient accents in red, green, and gold, and smooth hover animations.

Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this single-page template is fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. The navigation features scroll-spy functionality that highlights the active section as you scroll, plus a mobile hamburger menu for smaller screens. Perfect for holiday events, Christmas parties, family gatherings, or any festive countdown celebration. Free to download and customize for your personal or commercial projects.
