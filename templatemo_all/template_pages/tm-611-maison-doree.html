Maison Doree Template

Maison Doree is an elegant, free HTML CSS website template crafted for gold jewelry boutiques, luxury accessory brands, and fine jewelry stores. Featuring a sophisticated cream and antique gold color palette with Cormorant Garamond typography, this template delivers a premium boutique aesthetic without the typical AI-generated look. The responsive single-page design includes a hero section with rotating image slideshow, asymmetric collections gallery, brand story section, craftsmanship showcase with floating statistics, customer testimonials, and an appointment booking form - everything a jewelry business needs to establish a stunning online presence.

Built with pure HTML5 and CSS3, Maison Doree requires no frameworks or dependencies, making it lightweight, fast-loading, and easy to customize. The template is fully responsive across all devices from desktop monitors to mobile phones, with carefully crafted breakpoints ensuring your jewelry collections look beautiful on any screen. Perfect for jewelers, goldsmiths, bridal jewelry shops, and luxury retailers in the United States, United Kingdom, Europe, and Australia looking for a professional, free website template to showcase their exquisite pieces and attract discerning customers.
